# Fictional Company HR Policies  
## Company Name: Acme Tech Solutions

---

## 1. Leave Policy

### 1.1 Annual Leave
- Full-time employees are entitled to 18 paid leave days per calendar year.
- Leave accrues monthly.
- Unused leave may be carried forward up to 6 days.

### 1.2 Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 sick leave days per year.
- Medical certificate required if leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

### 1.3 Casual Leave
- 6 casual leave days per year.
- Cannot be carried forward.

---

## 2. Attendance & Work Hours

### 2.1 Work Hours
- Standard working hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM.
- Employees must work a minimum of 8 hours per day.

### 2.2 Late Arrival
- Up to 3 late arrivals per month are allowed.
- More than 3 may result in leave deduction.

---

## 3. Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

### 3.1 Eligibility
- Employees with more than 6 months tenure are eligible.
- Approval from reporting manager is mandatory.

### 3.2 Limits
- Maximum of 2 remote work days per week.
- Remote work is not allowed during notice period.

---

## 4. Holiday Policy

### 4.1 Company Holidays
- The company observes 10 fixed holidays per year.
- Holiday list is published annually by HR.

---

## 5. Code of Conduct

### 5.1 Professional Behavior
- Employees must maintain respectful conduct.
- Harassment, discrimination, or misuse of company resources is prohibited.

### 5.2 Disciplinary Action
- Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or termination.

---

## 6. Onboarding Policy

### 6.1 Joining Formalities
- Employees must submit all documents within 7 days of joining.
- Background verification is mandatory.

---

## 7. Exit Policy

### 7.1 Notice Period
- Notice period is 60 days for full-time employees.
- Notice period buyout is subject to manager and HR approval.
