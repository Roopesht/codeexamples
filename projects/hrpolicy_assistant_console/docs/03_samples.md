# Sample Interactions

---

### Example 1: Direct Policy Question

User:  
"How many annual leave days do I get?"

Assistant Response:
Answer: Full-time employees are entitled to 18 paid annual leave days per year.
Source Policy: Leave Policy → Annual Leave
Notes: Unused leave can be carried forward up to 6 days.
Confidence Level: High

---

### Example 2: Eligibility-Based Question

User:  
"Can I work from home full-time?"

Assistant Response:
Answer: Full-time remote work is not permitted under company policy.
Source Policy: Remote & Hybrid Work Policy → Limits
Notes: A maximum of 2 remote work days per week is allowed.
Confidence Level: High

---

### Example 3: Clarification Required

User:  
"Can I apply for remote work?"

Assistant Response:
Clarification Question:  
Are you asking as a full-time employee with more than 6 months tenure?

---

### Example 4: Out-of-Scope Question

User:  
"How is my salary calculated?"

Assistant Response:
Answer: This information is not defined in the HR policy documents.
Source Policy: Not applicable
Notes: Payroll calculations are outside the assistant’s scope.
Confidence Level: High

---

### Example 5: Ambiguous Policy Area

User:  
"What happens if I come late often?"

Assistant Response:
Answer: Up to 3 late arrivals per month are allowed without penalty.
Source Policy: Attendance & Work Hours → Late Arrival
Notes: More than 3 late arrivals may lead to leave deduction.
Confidence Level: Medium
